The Legend of Sleepy Hollow

written by Nathaniel Blackburn on 2022-02-12

Still pretty buzzed after this. The most important takeaways from this is that it's always good to heed the warnings of regional fables. Knowing ahead of time the limitations of occult things in the area may save one's life. Also knowing when to get the hell out of dodge may be worth not getting the beautiful girl at the end.
